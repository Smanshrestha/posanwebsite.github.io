Welcome to
Shree Pashupati Secondary School
Pashupati ma.vi is a goverment school for grade 1 to 12. We welcome students of all faiths,races, and background, offer various need-based scholarships each year. We have good and clean class rooms which enables staffs to build strong relationship with the children, know their families well and work teachers and parants together as a team for the benefit of the children in our care. Each student is fully supported at each stage of learning.

Pashupati mavi focus on integrity in technology , skills and value to teamwork in diversified community incorporating local brains with global ideas. All members of the school community will model the behaviors expected from the student. In doing this, the school will be a centre of excellence at the forefront of education, community development and environment awareness.
Message from Principle
Billy Butcher
It is both my pleasure and privilege to welcome you to pashupati ma vi , a school with a new vision. We successfully completed the 48th year of this school. We would like to extend our sincere gratitude to all who put their trust upon us. We are driven to provide best possible educational experience to all students. Our student come to us from different socio-political, economical ,cultural background, but they share common bonds: they value learning , respect each other and appreciate the skill and expertise of their teacher and friends. We believe that students are individual with rights and responsibilities therefore, we assist students to progressively accept more responsibility for self-discipline , to develop self respect , to contribute to the community , to develop a love of learning , and to strive for excellence.
