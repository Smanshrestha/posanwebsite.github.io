Shree Pashupati Secondary School
Bahradashi-3 Rajgadh (Jhapa)
Introduction
Pashupati Technical Secondary School was made for those children who will to study choosing the different courses . This school is located at Bahradashi -3 Jhapa.This school is government school and was established in 2019 B.S. .This school generates the students as a highly skilled manpower and provides them best qualification of education for working in different sectors.
We welcome and enroll student of all faith , races , and background , and offer various need-based scholarships each year. We have good and clean class rooms which enables staffs to build strong relationship with the children, know their families well and work teachers and parants together as a team for the benefit of the children in our care. Each student is fully supported at each stage of learning.
Vision statement
We are guided by "Teachers in School" and "Parents at Home" principle where staff, teacher,student and parents work together in a safe,caring and tolerant community to provide holistic enviroment with respect and dignity. This ensbles the student to achieve emotional , social,physical ,moral and spirtual development.
In this partnership, We focus on integrity in technology , skills and value to teamwork in diversified community incorporating local brains with global ideas. All members of the school community will model the behaviors expected from the students. In doing this , the school will be a centre of excellence at the forefront of education, community development and environment awareness.
Rules & Regulation
We believes that students need an ordered , structured environment and good discipline. School rules are based on courtesy , common sense and personal safety. The highest standards of behaviors and dress are expected and the school will deal firmly with students who behave unacceptably or fail to work satisfactorily.
The following rules are based on common sense and good practice:
¬Student should be polite and respectful to staffs , each others and visitors.
¬Student are expected to be punctual for school and lessons.
¬Full school uniform is to be worn.
¬Long hair,smoking is strictly forbidden.
Exam Policy
We integrate both the traditional formal exam system with modern thematic evaluation procedure through assessing individual performances. A series of class evaluation, observation of behavioral changes, performance in thematic assignments will be recorded and made available in the individual roster both manual and electonic forms for the parents. Every progress will be updated and parents can access to it in the school website through the unique ID number provided at the time of admission. The report cards of the student's progress will be sent after each formal exam. The result of the final exam conducted at the end of the acedemic session and the internal evaluation made throughout the year determine the upgrading of the child.
